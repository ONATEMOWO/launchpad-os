Project Overview

A practical workspace for student applications.

LaunchPad OS is a senior seminar project focused on helping students manage the work behind internships, scholarships, and research opportunities.

The public experience is intentionally simple: students can understand the purpose of the tool, create an account, and sign in to continue organizing their application materials.

Track

Keep each opportunity, deadline, and status in one place.

Prepare

Connect resumes, essays, transcripts, and references to the work that needs them.

Finish

Use checklists to see what is complete and what still needs attention.
