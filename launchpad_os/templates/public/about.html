Project Overview

A practical workspace for student applications.

LaunchPad OS helps students manage internships, scholarships, and research opportunities without scattering deadlines, materials, and notes across different tools.

Built for the first real workflow.

The current module focuses on opportunity tracking: students can create, update, archive, restore, and review the details of each opportunity they are pursuing.

Track

Keep title, organization, status, priority, and deadline in one place.

Review

Use detail pages to see links and notes without opening the edit form.

Archive

Move closed items out of active work while keeping the record available.
